Historical Census Browser

The data presented here describe the population and economy of U.S. states and counties from 1790 to 1960.

What can you do on this site?

  1. Browse census data for individual census years.
    • select specific categories of data within census years
    • produce tables of data by state or county
    • sort data by selected categories
    • create ratios between any two data categories
    • generate maps of selected data
  2. Examine state and county census data over time.
  3. Find out more about the data included in this site.
  4. Find links to further historical census resources.

Choose a census year to begin examining data by individual census year:

1790 1800 1810 1820 1830 1840 1850 1860 1870
1880 1890 1900 1910 1920 1930 1940 1950 1960

What you cannot do on this site:

  1. Find Information about individuals
  2. The site is not intended as a tool for downloading data for further research or more involved manipulation. Those who require this level of analysis should download the original data from ICPSR.
